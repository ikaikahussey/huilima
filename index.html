Hui Lima

Mutual Aid Coordination

We're collecting donations and organizing volunteers to support the residents in Waialua, Haleiwa, and elsewhere that are evacuating. List your donation sites and coordinate volunteer responses here. This is an open source website and we welcome additional functionality. Can also text with feedback: 808-221-2843.

Hui means "group" or "club" and lima means "hand" or "five" — together they represent hands working together.

County Emergency Alert Sign-Up Information

Honolulu (Oʻahu)

  • HNL Alert – Text HNLALERT to 888777 to opt in
  • Info: www.honolulu.gov/dem/hnl-alert

Kauaʻi County

  • Wireless Emergency Notification System (WENS)
  • Sign up online: entry.inspironlogistics.com/kauai_hi/wens.cfm

Maui County

  • MEMA Alerts (via CivicReady)
  • Sign up online: www.mauicounty.gov/list.aspx
  • Or text Mauialerts to 38276
  • Optional: Download the myAlerts mobile app (iOS/Android)

Hawaiʻi County (Big Island)

  • Blackboard Connect Emergency Notification System
  • Sign up: countyofhawaii.portal.finalsiteconnect.com
  • Text HAWAIIALERTS to 888777 to sign up for alerts
